## QUALIFICATION SCORING FRAMEWORK

### Scoring Criteria (1-5 each, max 25 points)

**ICP Segment Match (1-5)**
- 5: Perfect fit (Segment 1, 2, or 3)
- 4: Strong fit with minor gaps
- 3: Moderate fit, some alignment
- 2: Weak fit, major gaps
- 1: Poor fit, wrong segment

**Budget Alignment (1-5)**  
- 5: Enterprise budget ($3,350+ annually)
- 4: Business budget ($1,430-$3,350)
- 3: Starter/Pro budget ($470-$1,430)
- 2: Limited budget, price sensitive
- 1: Insufficient budget

**Decision Maker Accessibility (1-5)**
- 5: Direct access to CEO/founder
- 4: Access to VP/Head level
- 3: Access to manager level
- 2: Individual contributors only
- 1: No clear decision maker identified

**Competitive Landscape (1-5)**
- 5: No competing solution, greenfield
- 4: Non-competitive tools, easy switch
- 3: Partial overlap, positioning opportunity
- 2: Direct competitor, requires differentiation
- 1: Entrenched competitor, high switching cost

**Timing/Urgency (1-5)**
- 5: Urgent business trigger, immediate need
- 4: Recent trigger, timely opportunity
- 3: Moderate trigger, good timing
- 2: Weak trigger, lukewarm timing
- 1: No trigger, cold timing
