## Tier Grouping

### Tier 1 — Weeks 1-2: "Proof + Pipeline"
Hacks 1, 5, 7, 13, 2, 14, 6, 4. Can start today with zero infrastructure. Product IS the outreach asset. Agency tear sheet and GEO badge turn users into distributors. Reddit and Slack build authority while generating inbound. Comparison pages capture bottom-funnel search intent.

### Tier 2 — Weeks 3-4: "Expand Reach"
Hacks 15, 3, 10, 16, 9, 17, 8, 11. Require more setup — list building, data aggregation, booking lead time. Depend on Tier 1 producing proof points and testimonials.

### Tier 3 — Month 2+: "Systematize & Scale"
Hacks 18, 19, 12, 20. Infrastructure plays: onboarding drip, academy, Zapier integration, co-marketing. Each compounds over time but has higher build cost and slower payoff.
