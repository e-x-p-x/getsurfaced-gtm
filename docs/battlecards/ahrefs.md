## Why Surfaced Wins

### A | Purpose-Built GEO vs. Expensive Bolt-On
Ahrefs Brand Radar is an add-on to an SEO platform, starting at $828/mo total. Surfaced is purpose-built for GEO at €49/mo with more actionable features. The price difference is 15-17x for GEO-specific functionality.

### B | Accuracy Where It Matters
Real-world testing has found Brand Radar reports significantly fewer mentions than actually exist (3 ChatGPT mentions reported vs. 123 actual). Their keyword-based tracking rather than prompt-based tracking creates fundamental accuracy gaps.

### C | Gap Analysis + Content Generation
Ahrefs Brand Radar provides visibility data but no direction on how to improve it. No content generation, no gap analysis, no optimisation workflow. Surfaced goes from tracking to content in one platform.

## Their Weaknesses

**Accuracy Problems:**
- In real-world testing, Brand Radar reported only 3 ChatGPT mentions when actual data showed 123, and 6 Perplexity mentions versus 212 actual. "The foundational accuracy issues are impossible to ignore." ([Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review), [EWR Digital](https://www.ewrdigital.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review-alternatives-pricing-comparison/))
- Uses keyword-based tracking (predefined question datasets) rather than actual prompts people use — inherently limiting and inaccurate. ([Connor Kimball](https://connorkimball.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review-pricing-competitor-comparison/))
- Data sampling from a curated, rotating dataset — if your brand appears in prompts outside their sample set, those mentions go unrecorded. ([EWR Digital](https://www.ewrdigital.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review-alternatives-pricing-comparison/))

**Pricing:**
- $199/mo per individual AI platform index OR $699/mo for all 6 bundled. Plus mandatory base subscription ($129/mo minimum). Total: $828/mo minimum. "The astronomical price tag is impossible to ignore." ([EWR Digital](https://www.ewrdigital.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review-alternatives-pricing-comparison/), [Rankability](https://www.rankability.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review/))
- 15-17x more expensive than Surfaced for GEO functionality.

**Missing AI Platforms:**
- No Claude or Grok tracking — "a massive blind spot" given their rapidly growing user bases. ([Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review))

**No Actionable Insights:**
- "Brand Radar provides data on your brand's AI visibility but doesn't provide any direction to improve it, and there aren't AEO experts available to assist you." ([Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/ahrefs-brand-radar-review))
- No content generation, no gap analysis, no optimisation workflows.

## Trap Questions

1. **"Can you share accuracy benchmarks? How does Brand Radar's mention count compare to manual verification?"**
   - *Why it matters:* Independent testing found massive discrepancies (3 reported vs. 123 actual ChatGPT mentions).
   - *Good answer:* Transparent accuracy data with methodology.
   - *Bad answer:* "We track 243M+ prompts" — deflecting from accuracy to volume.

2. **"What's the total monthly cost for our team to track all AI platforms with Brand Radar?"**
   - *Why it matters:* $828/mo minimum ($129 base + $699 bundle). If they already have a higher-tier plan, the total is even more.
   - *Good answer:* Competitive, transparent pricing.
   - *Bad answer:* They calculate $828+ and the prospect does a double-take.

3. **"After Brand Radar shows us a visibility gap, what's the workflow to improve it — inside Ahrefs?"**
   - *Why it matters:* There isn't one. Brand Radar is monitoring only.
   - *Good answer:* Integrated content creation workflow.
   - *Bad answer:* "You'd use our Content Explorer or SEO Content Template" — which aren't connected to AI visibility data.

4. **"Your prompts are keyword-based. Do you track actual conversational AI prompts, or converted search queries?"**
   - *Why it matters:* Ahrefs uses predefined keyword-based questions, not real AI conversation prompts — fundamentally different data.
   - *Good answer:* "We capture real AI conversations."
   - *Bad answer:* "We curate and rotate a dataset of questions based on search patterns."
