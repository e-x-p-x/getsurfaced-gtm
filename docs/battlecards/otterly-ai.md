## Why Surfaced Wins

### A | Gap Analysis + Content Generation (Not Just Monitoring)
Otterly tells you where you're visible and where you're not. Surfaced goes further — it runs gap analysis between your website content and LLM responses, then generates optimised content to close the gaps. Otterly's users consistently cite "tells you what's happening but not what to do" as its biggest weakness. Surfaced solves this.

### B | Multilingual Content Operations
Surfaced supports multilingual content generation and optimisation workflows out of the box. Otterly has no content generation capabilities and no multilingual support — it's monitoring only.

### C | Professional Content Workflow
Surfaced provides prompt versioning, human-in-the-loop review, and automated quality scoring — a complete content production pipeline. Otterly is purely a monitoring dashboard with no content creation or quality control features.

## Their Weaknesses

**Feature Gaps:**
- No content generation, no gap analysis, no content optimisation workflows — monitoring only. Users must use separate tools to act on insights. ([PikaSEO](https://pikaseo.com/articles/otterly-ai-review))
- Missing Claude, Grok, DeepSeek tracking — 6-platform limit leaves blind spots. ([Multiple alternatives articles](https://getairefs.com/blog/best-otterly-ai-alternatives/))
- No tracking of underlying web searches that AI models execute — only tracks final output. ([LLM Pulse](https://llmpulse.ai/blog/best-otterly-ai-alternatives/))

**Pricing Complaints:**
- Steep pricing jumps: $29 → $189 → $989. Middle tier costs $1.89 per prompt, expensive at scale. ([Multiple review sources](https://getairefs.com/blog/best-otterly-ai-alternatives/))
- Prompt-based pricing makes large-scale monitoring unpredictable and expensive.

**Data Quality:**
- Weekly data refresh only — slower than competitors offering daily tracking. ([G2 reviews](https://www.g2.com/products/otterly-ai/reviews))
- No analytics integration — can't connect AI visibility data to website analytics or revenue.

**Team Size:**
- Small team (2–10 people) — raises questions about support capacity, development velocity, and enterprise reliability.

## Trap Questions

1. **"Can you show me a workflow for improving my AI visibility after I see the monitoring data?"**
   - *Why it matters:* Exposes the gap between monitoring and action. Otterly shows dashboards but has no content creation or optimisation workflow.
   - *Good answer:* They walk you through a specific workflow with actionable steps.
   - *Bad answer:* They suggest exporting data and using other tools — which is exactly the problem.

2. **"How often does the data refresh, and which AI platforms do you cover?"**
   - *Why it matters:* Weekly refresh and missing platforms (Claude, Grok, DeepSeek) are real limitations.
   - *Good answer:* Daily or real-time refresh across 8+ platforms.
   - *Bad answer:* "Weekly" and a list that doesn't include Claude, Grok, or DeepSeek.

3. **"If I need to generate content in multiple languages to improve my AI visibility, how does that work in your platform?"**
   - *Why it matters:* Otterly has no content generation and no multilingual support.
   - *Good answer:* Built-in multilingual content workflows.
   - *Bad answer:* "You'd need a separate content tool for that."

4. **"What does pricing look like if I need to track 200+ prompts across all platforms?"**
   - *Why it matters:* Prompt-based pricing makes Otterly expensive at scale. 200 prompts at $1.89/prompt = significant monthly cost.
   - *Good answer:* Flat or predictable pricing regardless of volume.
   - *Bad answer:* They calculate a number that surprises the prospect.
