# Content Engagement Outreach Sequence

## Trigger
Prospect liked, commented on, or shared content related to GEO, AI search optimisation, LLM visibility, or "how to show up in ChatGPT" — on LinkedIn, Reddit (r/SEO, r/bigseo, r/SEO_and_AI), or the GEO Community Slack (genengineoptimizers.com). They're actively learning about this space, which means the problem is top of mind.

## Target Persona
**Primary:** VP Marketing / Head of Content / Marketing Manager at SMEs (5–50 employees).
**Secondary:** Freelance SEO/GEO Consultants expanding into AI search services.
**Tertiary:** Digital Strategy Directors at agencies exploring GEO as a service line.

## Angle
- **Insight:** Engaging with GEO content means the prospect is either evaluating solutions or building internal knowledge. They're in research mode — the buying window is open but they haven't committed to a tool yet.
- **Bridge:** Surfaced is the tool that turns GEO research into action — from tracking AI visibility to generating the content that closes gaps. Perfect for someone who just learned what GEO is and needs a way to do it.
- **Opener:** Reference the specific piece of content they engaged with and add something they didn't already know. Position yourself as a peer who also found this interesting, not as a salesperson who saw an opportunity.
- **Ask:** "Worth comparing notes on what's actually working for GEO?"

## Sequence

### Day 1 — LinkedIn: Warm Engagement + Connection

**Connection note:**

{{first_name}}, saw your comment on [author]'s GEO post. Agreed — tracking AI visibility is the easy part. The hard part is knowing what content to create. We built a tool for exactly that.

**Tone note:** Peer-to-peer. You noticed the same content and have a shared interest. Not a cold pitch — a warm intro.

---

### Day 3 — Email: Value-First Follow-Up

**Subject:** That GEO post you engaged with — here's the missing piece
**Subject (A/B):** From GEO awareness to GEO action

{{first_name}},

Noticed you engaging with content about AI search optimisation recently. Most of what's out there covers the "why GEO matters" — but almost nothing covers the "how."

Here's what we've found works: run a gap analysis between what your website says and what LLMs actually tell people about your category. The delta is your content roadmap.

Surfaced automates that gap analysis and generates the content. Teams typically find 15-20 content gaps in their first audit.

Curious if you've started measuring your AI visibility yet?

**Objection addressed:** "We already have a solution" — preempted by distinguishing between monitoring (which others do) and gap analysis + content generation (which Surfaced does).

**Tone note:** Educational and curious. You're sharing an insight, then asking a genuine question.

---

### Day 6 — LinkedIn: DM with Resource

**DM (if connected):**

{{first_name}}, quick thing — we published a breakdown of the 3 content types that earn the most AI citations. Thought you'd find it useful given your interest in GEO. Happy to share if you're interested.

**Tone note:** Giving, not asking. The resource is the value. If they say yes, you have a warm conversation started.
