# GEO Visibility Gap Outreach Sequence

## Trigger
Company's brand is visibly absent from LLM responses in their category. When you query ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews for their product category, competitors appear but they don't. This is Surfaced's strongest buying signal — you have concrete proof of the problem before the first message.

## Target Persona
**Primary:** VP Marketing / Head of Content / CEO at SMEs (5–50 employees) in SaaS, e-commerce, fintech, or B2B tech.
**Secondary:** Agency Owner / Digital Strategy Director at digital marketing agencies (10–100 employees) who manage client brands with the same gap.

## Angle
- **Insight:** The prospect's competitors are already appearing in AI-generated answers for category-relevant prompts. The prospect is invisible. Every day this continues, prospects who ask AI for recommendations are being sent to competitors instead.
- **Bridge:** Surfaced runs gap analysis between a brand's website and what LLMs actually say — then generates the content needed to close the gap. This is the exact workflow needed to go from invisible to cited.
- **Opener:** A screenshot or specific example of the AI query where the prospect's competitor appears and they don't. This is inarguable proof — not a pitch, but a fact.
- **Ask:** "Want me to send the full gap report?"

## Sequence

I asked ChatGPT "what are the best tools for [prospect's category]?" Your top 3 competitors were mentioned. You weren't.

This isn't an SEO problem — it's a GEO problem. LLMs pull from structured content, citations, and authority signals that are different from what Google ranks.

We built Surfaced to find exactly what's missing from your site vs. what LLMs are saying, then generate the content to close that gap.

Want me to send the full gap analysis for your brand?

**Objection addressed:** "We already do SEO" — preempted by distinguishing GEO from SEO explicitly.

**Tone note:** Matter-of-fact, not alarmist. You're sharing a discovery, not making a threat.
