# Hiring Signal Outreach Sequence

## Trigger
Company posts a job listing for an SEO Manager, Content Lead, Content Marketing Manager, or Head of Growth — with mentions of AI, GEO, LLM optimisation, or "AI search" in the job description. This signals they're scaling content operations and will hit tooling decisions within 30–60 days of the hire starting.

## Target Persona
**Primary:** VP Marketing / CEO who posted the role — the person making tooling decisions before the new hire arrives.
**Secondary:** The hiring manager who will need to equip the new hire with the right stack on day one.

## Angle
- **Insight:** Hiring a content or SEO role with AI/GEO requirements means two things: (1) they've identified a gap in their current team, and (2) the new hire will need tools on day one. The 30–60 day window between job posting and the new hire's first tooling evaluation is the prime outreach window.
- **Bridge:** Surfaced is the tool the new hire will need — GEO tracking, gap analysis, and content generation in one platform. Getting it set up before the hire starts means faster onboarding and immediate productivity.
- **Opener:** Reference the specific job listing and the AI/GEO requirements mentioned in it. Show you read the JD, not just the title.
- **Ask:** "Want me to set up a free workspace so your new hire has GEO data ready on day one?"

## Objection Playbook

| Objection | Response | Used in |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| "Not the right time — we haven't hired yet" | The best time to set up tooling is before the hire starts. It accelerates their onboarding and shows you're ahead of the curve. | Day 1 email |
| "We already have SEO tools" | SEO tools don't cover GEO — different signals, different content types. Your new hire will evaluate GEO-specific tools regardless. | Day 7 email |
| "Too expensive / exploring options" | Free tier — 10 minutes to set up. No commitment. Your new hire can evaluate it alongside anything else they test. | Day 7 + Day 14 emails |
| "Need to involve the new hire" | Exactly — bookmark this for their first week. The free workspace with baseline data gives them a head start. | Day 14 email |
